Chilli Con Carne

Chilli con carne recipe

This great chilli recipe has to be one of the best dishes to serve to friends for a casual get-together. An easy sharing favourite that uses up storecupboard ingredients. It's a hearty homemade favourite, super easy to prepare & a great choice for batch cooking.
